What it does

Everything your
bedtime needs.

Six features that work together to make winding down effortless — all running quietly in the background, even when the app is fully closed.

01
Stops your music

Stops audio from any app at your set bedtime — Spotify, YouTube, podcasts, all of it.

02
Do Not Disturb

Enables DND at bedtime and restores your normal settings in the morning automatically.

03
Dims your screen

Reduces brightness to minimum at bedtime. Restored each morning so you start fresh.

04
Morning alarm

PhaseOut fires your alarm and restores all settings — brightness, sound, everything.

05
Usage analytics

Per-app time tracking, daily limits, and weekly trend charts at a glance.

06
Focus mode

Block every app except the ones you choose. A gentle overlay nudges you back on track.

How it works

Set it once.
Runs every night.

PhaseOut runs as a background service — reliably, even when the app is fully closed.

  1. 01
    Create a bedtime schedule

    Choose a time, the days it repeats, and which actions to run. Takes under a minute.

  2. 02
    Get a heads-up reminder

    30 minutes before, PhaseOut sends a gentle notification so you can wrap up.

  3. 03
    Bedtime runs automatically

    Audio stops, DND kicks in, screen dims — all without you touching your phone.

  4. 04
    Morning alarm restores everything

    Your alarm fires, brightness and sound return to normal. Your day starts clean.
