01

The short version

PhaseOut collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything the app does — schedules, usage stats, focus sessions, settings — lives entirely on your device and never leaves it. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that phone home.

The sections below explain this in detail as required by applicable privacy regulations. If anything is unclear, contact us at the address at the bottom of this page.

02

Information we collect

We collect no information. PhaseOut does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on any server operated by BrightDev or any third party.

All data generated by your use of the app — including your bedtime schedules, daily screen time figures, app usage breakdowns, and focus session history — is stored locally in your device's private app storage using SQLite (sqflite). This data is inaccessible to BrightDev and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

03

Permissions we request

PhaseOut requests the following Android permissions solely to deliver its core functionality. None of these permissions are used to collect data about you.

  • Usage Access — to read per-app screen time from the Android UsageStatsManager so the app can display your usage analytics locally.
  • Do Not Disturb Access — to enable and disable DND mode at your scheduled bedtime and restore it in the morning.
  • Write Settings — to adjust screen brightness at bedtime and restore it each morning.
  • Schedule Exact Alarms — to fire your morning alarm and bedtime triggers at the exact times you specify.
  • Foreground Service / Boot Receiver — to keep the background scheduler alive reliably, including after device restart.
  • Draw Over Other Apps (optional) — used only in Focus Mode to display a gentle overlay when a blocked app is opened.

No permission is used to collect, transmit, or store data remotely.

04

Third-party services

PhaseOut does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs. There are no calls to Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Sentry, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any comparable service.

The app may use Firebase for update notifications only if you have consented to in-app update checks. This is limited to version metadata retrieval and does not involve any user identifier or usage data.

05

Children's privacy

PhaseOut does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, the app is safe for use by people of all ages.

06

Data retention & deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can clear it at any time through Android's standard app settings (Settings → Apps → PhaseOut → Storage → Clear Data). Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

BrightDev holds no copy of your data and therefore cannot retrieve, export, or delete it on your behalf — it was never sent to us in the first place.

07

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Material changes will be communicated via a notice inside the app. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

Our commitment — that PhaseOut will never collect personal data — will not change.
