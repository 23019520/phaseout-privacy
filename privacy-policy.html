PhaseOut

Sleep & Focus · Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PhaseOut is built by BrightDev. This policy explains what data the app accesses, what it does with that data, and what it will never do.

PhaseOut does not collect, upload, or sell your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data we access on your device

PhaseOut reads the following data locally to provide its features:

  • App usage statistics — how long you spend in each app each day, used to display screen time and enforce limits you set yourself
  • Battery level and charging state — used to predict when your battery will reach 20%, recalculated daily from your own device's history
  • Notification access — used only to detect active media sessions and stop audio at your scheduled bedtime
  • Screen brightness — read and written only when you enable the dim brightness bedtime option
  • Do Not Disturb status — toggled only when you enable the DND bedtime option; your previous setting is restored in the morning

Data that stays on your device

All data PhaseOut creates or accesses is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. This includes your schedules, daily usage records, battery snapshots, focus sessions, and activity logs.

None of this is transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

Network access

PhaseOut does not make network requests during normal operation. The only network activity is Firebase Crashlytics, which sends anonymous crash reports when the app encounters an error. These reports contain the type of error, your device model, Android version, and app version number. They do not contain usage data, schedule contents, or any personally identifying information.

You can disable crash reporting at any time in Settings → Privacy → Crash analytics.

Permissions we request

PhaseOut requests permissions only when you first use the feature that needs them — not all at once on first open.

  • Notifications — to send bedtime reminders and schedule alerts
  • Usage Access — to show per-app screen time
  • Notification Listener — to stop media from any app at bedtime
  • Display over other apps — to show the focus lock overlay
  • Battery optimisation exemption — to keep the background service alive overnight
  • Do Not Disturb access — only if you enable the DND bedtime option
  • Modify system settings — only if you enable the brightness dim option
  • Set exact alarms — to schedule the morning alarm

What we never do

  • Collect or upload your app usage data
  • Read your messages, contacts, photos, or files
  • Share data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Track your location
  • Show advertisements
  • Require account creation
  • Sell your data in any form

Children's privacy

PhaseOut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will assist with data removal.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice on the next launch. Continued use of PhaseOut after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
