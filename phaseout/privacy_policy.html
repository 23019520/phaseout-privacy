Privacy Policy

PhaseOut is a sleep and digital wellness app built by BrightDev. This policy explains clearly what data PhaseOut accesses, what it does with that data, and what it never does.

The short version: PhaseOut does not collect, upload, or sell your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

📊 Data we access on your device

PhaseOut reads the following data locally on your device to provide its features:

  • App usage statistics — how long you spend in each app each day, used to display your screen time and enforce usage limits you set
  • Battery level and charging state — used to predict when your battery will reach 20%, updated each session
  • Notification access — used only to detect active media sessions and stop audio playback at your scheduled bedtime
  • Screen brightness — read and written only when you enable the "Dim brightness" bedtime option
  • Do Not Disturb status — toggled only when you enable the DND bedtime option; your previous setting is restored in the morning

🔒 Data that stays on your device

All data that PhaseOut accesses or creates is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. This includes:

  • Your schedules and their settings
  • Daily app usage records
  • Battery snapshots used for prediction
  • Focus session history
  • Schedule activity logs

None of this data is transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

🌐 Network access

PhaseOut does not make network requests during normal operation. The only network activity is Firebase Crashlytics, which sends anonymous crash reports when the app encounters an error. These reports contain:

  • The type of error and where in the code it occurred
  • Your device model and Android version
  • App version number

Crash reports do not contain usage data, schedule contents, or any personally identifying information. You can disable analytics in Settings → Privacy → Usage analytics.

🔔 Permissions we request

PhaseOut requests permissions contextually — only when you first use the feature that needs them, not all at once on first open.

  • Notifications — to send bedtime reminders and schedule alerts
  • Usage Access — to show your per-app screen time
  • Notification Listener — to stop media from any app at bedtime
  • Display over other apps — to show the focus lock overlay
  • Battery optimisation exemption — to keep the background service alive overnight
  • Do Not Disturb access — only if you enable the DND bedtime option
  • Modify system settings — only if you enable the brightness dim option
  • Set exact alarms — to schedule the morning alarm

🚫 What we never do

  • We never collect or upload your app usage data
  • We never read your messages, contacts, photos, or files
  • We never share data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We never track your location
  • We never show ads
  • We never require account creation
  • We never sell your data in any form

👶 Children's privacy

PhaseOut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will assist with data removal.

🔄 Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice on the next app launch. Continued use of PhaseOut after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
